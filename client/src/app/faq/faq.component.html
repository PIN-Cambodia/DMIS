Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers to your most common questions

What is DMIS?

This Disaster Management Information System provides a centralised repository for the systematic collection, documentation, analysis and display of relevant data related to the preparedness, detection and response to natural and man made disasters. The DMIS is a tool that helps to provide alerting and analysis functionality to members of the disaster response community in Cambodia, in order to better prepare communities and reduce the impact of disasters. The Disaster Management Information System was developed by thinkWhere in partnership with People in Need on behalf of the World Food Programme for use by the National Committee for Disaster Management in Cambodia and other Civil Society Organisations.

What data is available through DMIS?

There are various datasets provided in three different categories: Preparadness, Incidents and Warnings, Assessment and Response. Mapping data provides information on Cambodian demographics, infrastructure, weather conditions and other.
